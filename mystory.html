Welcome to MY STORY

Master Chef at 14

Abandoned at the door of a small backstreet cafe in Korea, Chef Red, or as he was known then as Jimni, was adopted by the owners and brought up as their only son.

Chef Red learned everything he could from his Mama and started to evolve his own style.

Chef Red went on to become one of the forefathers of ‘Haute Cuisine’ in Korea.

Flambe Cooking

At the tender age of 13 he was weilding knives and cooking over huge flames, concocting a vast array of beautiful mouthwatering meals.

He started to get attention and people came from far and wide to get a glimpse of his flamboyant style and a taste of that mythical cooking.
